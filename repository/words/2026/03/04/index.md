title: Tripling my computer monitors
---

Over the holidays I received a "home office" upgrade, and I'm loving it.

Before I was doing all my work on a laptop. One screen, lots of command-tabbing, and constant context switching. It technically works, but it wasn't exactly efficient.

I’m now using a laptop monitor extension that adds two extra 1080p screens. Having three displays means I can keep apps such as email, chat, or docs open all the time and stay focused in my editor. Less bouncing around, more actual coding.
