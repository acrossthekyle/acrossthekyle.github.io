title: About that daily step goal
---
Winter in Chicago makes it much harder to hit a daily step goal. Walking the dog helps, but it usually doesn't quite get me there.

Enter the indoor portable elliptical and zoning out to TV while knocking out the last few thousand steps. It works...most days. And on the days when the motivation just isn't there? That's fine too.

Here's hoping for warmer weather soon, and more early morning walks along the lakefront.
